About Us

Madarasa-E-Noore Ilahi Niswan is a dedicated Islamic learning center for girls and women, committed to nurturing faith, knowledge, and character. We provide quality Arabic and Islamic education in a safe, respectful, and spiritually uplifting environment.

Here, every girl is guided to grow in deen, strengthen her values, and become a light for her family and community.
